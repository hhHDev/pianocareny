What we do

All Piano Care services are provided by a professional Piano Tuner and Technician. We offer:

  • Tuning
  • Repair
  • Regulation
  • Restoration
  • Voicing
  • Dampp Chaser Installation

You may be surprised at how good your piano can sound and how much more enjoyable the piano is to play when given a fine professional tuning. At Piano Care, our specialty is bringing out the special voice of your piano, whatever its age or condition.

Who we are

Piano Care is a tuning and piano repair service based in Queens, NY and is owned and operated by Jake Gonchar, a Registered Piano Technician (RPT) and member of the Piano Technicians Guild. As a professional piano technician, Jake is qualified to tune, repair, regulate and restore your piano, using fine craftsmanship and the precision techniques he was taught at the esteemed North Bennett Street School in Boston, Massachusetts, and has since honed with hard work and dedication.

What We've Done

Piano Care has tuned, repaired, and serviced pianos belonging to everyone from professional musicians to parents looking to give a child their first lesson. Recording studios, churches, concert venues, senior centers, piano shops and dealerships, public and private schools, apartments, and houses: if there is a piano and it is tunable, we can tune it and tune it well. It doesn't matter if it's a Steinway D, or a Winter Spinet, the time and effort servicing every piano is made with the respect and proficiency that is necessary to do the job.

Where we work

Piano Care is based in Queens, in Astoria, New York. We service all of NYC, Bronx, Brooklyn, Manhattan, Queens, and Staten Island, parts of Long Island, and Westchester County. If you're not sure whether we work in your area, just give us a call and ask!
